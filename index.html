Important disclaimer

This tool is a calculation aid only. It does not determine award coverage, correct classification, annualised wage clause compliance, set-off enforceability, record-keeping compliance, or all possible overtime/penalty/allowance obligations. Verify against the applicable award, contract and current Fair Work guidance before relying on the result.
